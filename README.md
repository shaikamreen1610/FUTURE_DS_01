# FUTURE_DS_01
# SuperStore Sales Analysis|Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales data from a Super Store using **Power BI**.
The dashboard helps understand how the business is performing across **sales, profit, customers, regions, categories, and time**. It is designed to support better business decisions through clear and interactive visuals.

---

## Project Goals

* Understand overall **sales and profit performance**
* Identify **top-selling categories and sub-categories**
* Analyze **region-wise and state-wise sales**
* Track **monthly sales and profit trends**
* Study **shipping mode usage**
* Measure **customer and order behavior**

---

## Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and dashboard creation
* **Excel** – Data cleaning and preparation

---

##  Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** 2.30M
* **Total Profit:** 286.40K
* **Total Orders:** 5,009
* **Total Customers:** 793
* **Average Order Value:** 458.61

---

##  Dashboard Summary

### Sales Analysis

* **Sales by Category and Sub-Category**
  Technology, especially Phones, generates the highest revenue.

* **Sales by Region**
  The West and East regions contribute the most to total sales.

* **Sales and Profit by State (Map View)**
  Shows which states perform better and where sales are concentrated.

---

### Trend Analysis

* **Monthly Sales Trend**
  Sales increase toward the end of every year, indicating seasonal demand.

* **Monthly Profit Trend**
  Helps track profitability across different time periods.

---

### Customer & Order Insights

* **Sales by Shipping Mode**
  Standard Class is the most commonly used shipping option.

---

## Key Insights

* Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
* Sales show clear seasonal growth patterns.
* The West region leads in overall sales.
* Standard Class shipping is preferred by most customers.
* Certain states consistently outperform others in sales.
